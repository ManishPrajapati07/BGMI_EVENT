About the Event

The BGMI College Gaming Tournament is a competitive e-sports event organized by our college gaming community to encourage students to participate in professional-style gaming competitions and showcase their strategic and teamwork skills.

This event provides a platform for young gamers to experience real-time competitive gameplay in a friendly and disciplined environment. Teams from different departments will compete against each other in intense battle royale matches to prove their dominance and earn the title of College Champions.

Apart from entertainment, the tournament aims to promote coordination, leadership, decision-making, and communication skills among students. It also spreads awareness about e-sports as a growing career opportunity in the field of digital sports and online gaming.

The event will be conducted under fair play rules with proper monitoring to ensure transparency and equal opportunity for all participants. Winners will receive trophies, certificates, and exciting prizes.
